Use an alternate vehicle authorization profile Recommended — keeps your main BYD account isolated. Tap to view setup steps.
  1. Open the official BYD app on your phone.
  2. Go to Vehicle Authorization.
  3. Tap New Authorization.
  4. Enter a secondary email — it must be different from your main account email.
  5. Choose an authorization period that fits how long you want this profile to stay active.
  6. Log out of the BYD app, then sign up with the secondary email using the verification code and create a password.
  7. Tap Lock or Unlock in the BYD app — it will prompt you to set a Control Pin.
  8. Enter the secondary email, password, and Control Pin here in OverDrive.
